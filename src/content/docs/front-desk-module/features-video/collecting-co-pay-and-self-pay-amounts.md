### Step 1: Hi, this video shows how you can collect the co pay amount and how you can collect the self pay amount for a patient.  To collect the co pay amount, click on the appointment card.

### Step 2: Go to the billing info section.

### Step 3: The copay amount has auto populated.  Enter that copay amount here.

### Step 4: Click collect payment.

### Step 5: Select the payment method.

### Step 6: Click collect payment and your payment will be collected successfully.

### Step 7:

### Step 8: You will be able to see that the amount paid is $35.  Next, for the self pay amount.  Click on the appointment card.

### Step 9: Go to the billing info section.

### Step 10: You can see the total charge for the appointment is $70.  Select the amount to be collect it.

### Step 11: Click Collect payment.

### Step 12: You can send out an invoice to the patient from here or you can collect the payment here itself.

### Step 13: Select the payment method click Collect payment.

### Step 14:

### Step 15: You can see that the collected amount has been updated.  If you go over the appointment card you can see that the amount has been updated here as well.
