In this article, you'll learn how to cancel recurring appointments in two different ways.

### Step 1: Start by clicking on the dots shown in the image below.

### Step 2: Find and click on the Cancel appointment option.

Alternatively, you can click directly on the appointment to cancel it.

### Step 3: After clicking on the appointment,

### Step 4: Choose the reason for cancellation.

### Step 5: Now, you'll see the Cancel recurring appointments option.

### Step 6: Click on it.

### Step 7: Next, select Appointments.

### Step 8: You'll have the option to select all appointments or select a particular one.

### Step 9: Choose Recurring appointments.
