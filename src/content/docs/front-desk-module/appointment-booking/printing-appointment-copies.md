In this article, you'll learn how to print a paper copy of your appointments. 

### Step 1: Start by clicking on the three dots on your appointment card.

### Step 2: Next, select Print and Download.

### Step 3: Choose whether you want to print all appointments or only future appointments.

### Step 4: Decide on the items you want to be printed.

### Step 5: Now, select Print or Download.

### Step 6: Note that the columns are editable.

### Step 7: Finally, click on Print to get your paper copy.
