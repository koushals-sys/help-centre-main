In this article, you'll learn how to view and respond to patient messages received via SMS from the clinic.

### Step 1: Look for a message notification on your screen when a patient receives an SMS from the clinic. It should look something like this:

### Step 2: Click on the notification to view the patient's message.

### Step 3: Click on Reply.

### Step 4: This action takes you to the emitter dashboard where you'll see all messages from the patient. From here, you're able to respond.

### Step 5: After typing your response, click on the Send button. The patient will receive your reply and it will be displayed on your screen.
