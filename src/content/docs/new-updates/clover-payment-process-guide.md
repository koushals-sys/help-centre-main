In this article, you'll learn how to use Clover Inspiring for both terminal and online payments.

### Step 1: Start by observing the Clover terminal and Clover Online interface.

### Step 2: The terminal is designed for offline payments. Simply tap or swipe your card to initiate the payment process.

### Step 3: For online payments, notice the save card option. If you'd like to save your card for future transactions, select this option.

### Step 4: To proceed with the payment, input your card number, expiration date, and CVV. Once you've entered these details, the payment processing will begin.

### Step 5: Upon successful payment, you'll see a confirmation message. That's it! You've now learned how to use Clover for terminal and online payments.
