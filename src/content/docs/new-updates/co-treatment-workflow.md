In this article, you'll learn how to add CO treatment to a SOAP Note and view the treatment summary.

### Step 1: Start by clicking on the SOAP Note where you want to add CO treatment.

### Step 2: Proceed to fill the subjective section.

### Step 3: Next, fill the objective section.

### Step 4: Continue with the assessment workflow.

### Step 5: Now, you're in the planning section.

### Step 6: Here, locate the CO treatment button.

### Step 7: Activate the toggle.

### Step 8: Scroll down to the cross procedure section. Under this section, you'll find two options: one for the therapist and one for the therapist assistant.

### Step 9: Add the minute. Once you do this, the unit will be up.

### Step 10: Ensure Auto Popul is active.

### Step 11: This step is crucial if you want to see the treatment summary.

### Step 12: To view the treatment summary, click on the Billing section.

### Step 13: Here, you'll see the CPT codes and the modifiers for the patients.
