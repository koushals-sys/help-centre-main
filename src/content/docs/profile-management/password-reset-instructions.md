### Step 1: How to reset your password?  Click on "Forgot password."

### Step 2: Enter your username, which would be your email ID from which you log in.

### Step 3: Click on submit.

### Step 4: The email will be triggered to your registered email ID. Click on the password reset link.

### Step 5: It'll take you to this screen.  Enter your new password.  Confirm the new password.

### Step 6: Click on reset, and your password will be changed successfully.
