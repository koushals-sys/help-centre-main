In this article, you'll learn how to use the updated features of the Spry HEP effectively.

### Step 1: Navigate to the HEP tab.

### Step 2: Use the search bar to filter by specialties.

### Step 3: Mark your favorite exercises.

### Step 4: Create programs using your favorite exercises.

### Step 5: Search for specific programs and exercises within your documentation.

### Step 6: Select exercises for your program.

### Step 7: Choose either all exercises

or individual exercises.

### Step 8: Search for exercises by most recent use.

### Step 9: Access your favorite exercises.

### Step 10: Use custom exercises you've added.

### Step 11: Rearrange the order of the selected exercises.

### Step 12: Include the rearranged exercises in the HEP.

### Step 13: Apply the same steps for Clinic exercises within the HEP.

### Step 14: Search by programs, most recent favorites, and custom exercises.

### Step 15: Filter exercises by body parts and specialty.

### Step 16: Rearrange and reorder the exercises as needed.
