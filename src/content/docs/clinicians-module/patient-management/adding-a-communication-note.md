In this article, you'll learn how to add a Communication Note to a patient's profile. 

### Step 1: Navigate to the patient's profile.

### Step 2: Click on View Profile to access the Patient Timeline.

### Step 3: Locate and click on the Communication Note option.

### Step 4: A new window for your Communication Note will open.

### Step 5: Choose a subject for your note. In this example, we're selecting Reschedule.

### Step 6: Save your note by clicking on Save.

### Step 7: Your Communication Note is now saved. You'll find it on the left side of the scroll view.

### Step 8: Click on your saved Communication Note to view it. From here, you're also able to download the note.

### Step 9: To see the most recent Communication Note, navigate to the Timeline.

### Step 10: Here, you'll find the latest Communication Note.

And that's it! You've successfully added a Communication Note to a patient's profile. Thank you for following along.
