In this article, you'll learn how to add your insurance card details.

### Step 1: Navigate to the patient section.

### Step 2: Find the operation name.

### Step 3: Click on View Profile.

### Step 4: Scroll down and select Insurance Car.

### Step 5: Click on Add new insurance card.

### Step 6: Upload the front side of your insurance card.

### Step 7: Review your insurance card details or let them auto-populate.

That's it! You've successfully added your insurance card details.
