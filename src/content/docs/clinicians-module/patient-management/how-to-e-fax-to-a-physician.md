### Step 1: This video demonstrates how to e-fax a patient to a physician. Go to the patient dashboard.

### Step 2: You can view here or search for the patient name.

### Step 3: Once you get the name, View profile.

### Step 4: You have reached the patient's timeline.

### Step 5:   Now scroll down and go to the POC section

### Step 6:  Click on eFax option.

### Step 7: You can add the physician's number directly or select them from the dropdown if they're already in the system.

### Step 8: Now add the subject and add comments.

### Step 9:  As you can see, Plan of care adds up as an attachment.

### Step 10:  Now click on send.
