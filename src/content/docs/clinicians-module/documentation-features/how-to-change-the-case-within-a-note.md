In this article, you'll learn how to change the case within a note. 

### Step 1: Navigate to the SOAP notes.

### Step 2: View the SOAP notes.

### Step 3: Click on the SOAP note you want to change.

### Step 4: Look for the case you want to modify.

### Step 5: Click on the plus sign to create a new case.

### Step 6: Enter the case title.

### Step 7: Select the specialty for the case.

### Step 8: Choose the body part related to the case.

### Step 9: Fill in the mandatory details.

### Step 10: Click on "Create" to create the new case.

### Step 11: Click on "S" to save your changes.

Your case with the new updates is now ready. Thank you for following this tutorial.
